Use this form to send your questions about what information is expected in the declaration of performance of your product when selling it in Greece. Please specify the product and applicable harmonised standard using the table of harmonised standards at the tab “legislation/ harmonised standards” and ask your question in Greek or English. You should expect an answer within 15 working days.
